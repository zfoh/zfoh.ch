Roman Kashitsyn - Embracing the Failure

Thursday 25 October, 19:00

Continuing the theme of unit testing, we’ll discuss why it’s important to observe unit test failures and how to make this experience less disturbing. I will also present the design of the library I started to work on recently in order to make unit testing in Haskell a bit less painful. We’ll take a look at how the types started simple and then gradually evolved to accommodate new requirements.

This talk should be easily approachable even for people just starting their journey into Haskell but could also be interesting for seasoned Haskellers.
